Technical features

Perfectly tailored

Having the option of 100+ combinations gives you the freedom to get exactly what your body needs to stay on top of the game.

Sustainable

We not only enable you to ditch the plastic bottle but we also make use of local water to reduce transportation emissions. Make a measurable impact with every drink!

Easy Servicing

We ensure efficient operation via our IoT-Platform. Timely alerts as well as step-by-step explanations for task, ensure that you can focus on what you’re best at.

Swiss quality

Our dispenser is assembled and designed in Switzerland ensuring that transportation ways are minimized and quality standards are fulfilled.

Customer engagement

Use our dispenser to get feedback from your employees, promote an upcoming event or keep them informed. You have the freedom to play our display as you wish.

Flexibility

Install the fountain in your existing infrastructure or get it as a stand-alone version. The fountain is designed with you in mind.

Installation requirements

Requirements

  • Water supply (⅜” outer diameter)
  • Standard 220 V outlet
  • Standard Swiss/European counter
  • Centered cutout in counter, directly over the placement of chiller (template provided)
  • Wifi (if possible)
  • Waste water connection (optional).

Dimensions

  • Table Top Version: W 200 mm x D 250mm x H 460 mm
  • Stand-up Version: W 600 mm x D 500mm x H 1450 mm
