Benefits at a glance

Revolutionize the beverage consumption in your office

Ditch plastic bottles by simply refilling

Our fountain enables you to choose an alternative to plastic bottles. You can conveniently fill up flavoured drinks in your reusable bottle or cup, cutting CO2-Emissions by more than 50%. Become a part of the sustainable future by going waste-free.

Cut your cost by 50% on average

Designed for low maintenance and automated reordering the fountain saves offices on average 50% of their beverage expenses and let’s you focus on what you are best at. Less packaging also means less expenses - so you only pay for what you really get.

Offer Healthy & tasty drinks

Tailor the drink to your specific needs by choosing the type of water, one of our natural flavours and add from up to 2 functional enhancement to get you ready for your tasks ahead. Ready to experience your full potential every single day?

Our Journey to reduce 1 billion PET bottles

The idea of founding a company together to fight the extensive use of single-use plastic came up at a family dinner. Luca told Alex about his recent pain point that every time he wants a flavoured drink while commuting, he relies on a single-use plastic bottle even though he has his reusable bottle with him.

In the following month Alex recognized the extent of the problem at a Beach Clean-up in Ghana and once he returned, he was very excited to start project “Fountain” and tackle the plastic problem.

Eager to make a change we started building our first prototypes and tested them with different organizations where it became evident that we are not the only ones ready for a change towards more circularity.
