OpenClaw Gmail Assistant

Effective date: April 1, 2026

Overview

OpenClaw Gmail Assistant is a personal automation tool used to streamline Gmail and Google Calendar workflows. This app is built for personal use.

Data Access and Use

  • The app only accesses Google data needed to perform requested email and scheduling tasks.
  • Data is used solely to provide the app's functionality.
  • Data is not sold or shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Storage and Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed to operate and maintain the app's workflows. Access tokens and related credentials are handled with reasonable security practices.

User Control

You can revoke app access at any time through your Google account permissions. If you have any privacy-related questions, contact: alexander.pfyffer@gmail.com.
